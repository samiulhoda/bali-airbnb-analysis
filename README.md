# 🏝 Bali Airbnb Market Analysis (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Airbnb listings in Bali (Feb 2025 – Jan 2026) to identify revenue drivers, pricing strategies, and booking behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand key factors driving revenue
* Analyze pricing vs occupancy trends
* Identify high-performing property types
* Provide business recommendations

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Total revenue: **$366M**
* ADR ($121) contributes more than occupancy (~29%)
* Entire homes & villas generate ~52% of revenue
* Pricing strategy impacts revenue more than demand volume
* Average lead time (~44 days) enables dynamic pricing opportunities

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimize pricing strategies instead of focusing only on occupancy
* Target high-performing segments (villas, entire homes)
* Use booking lead time for dynamic pricing
* Align pricing with seasonal demand trends
